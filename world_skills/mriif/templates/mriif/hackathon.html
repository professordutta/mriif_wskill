Introduction

In an era of rapidly advancing technology, Extended Reality (XR) is transforming the way we learn, teach, and engage with content. To accelerate the adoption of XR in education and Skill development, the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) will be organising National XR Learning Hackathon.

This initiative seeks to identify top-performing institutions/Students across India to spearhead XR content development using the cutting-edge EON Reality Platform.

The goal is to empower students to create immersive learning experiences that align with India's vision for a technologically empowered future.

Key Features

Purpose and Objectives

  • Promote awareness and adoption of XR Learning in India
  • Foster innovation in educational/skill content development
  • Identify and support top academic institutions
  • Build a sustainable ecosystem of XR-enabled learning solutions

Eligibility

  • AICTE Affiliated Institutions
  • UGC Affiliated Institutions
  • DGT Affiliated Institutions
  • Vocational Training Institutes

Hackathon Structure

Registration Process

  • One Institute may register up to 10 Teams per Category
  • Each Team comprises 4 Students and 1 Faculty Member
  • Teams submit proposals addressing educational/skill challenges

Evaluation Process

  • Expert panel evaluation of proposals
  • Shortlisted teams receive XR platform licenses
  • Comprehensive online training provided

Stakeholder Engagement

NSDC

Policy design and overall supervision of the hackathon implementation.

MREI

Technical and operational assistance, including registration and training management.

EON XR

Technical and subject matter expert support for implementation.

Calendar

Event Timeline
Release of Guidelines Jan 2025
Registration Period Jan – March 2025
National Hackathon May 2025

Rules and Regulations

Evaluation Criteria

  • Creativity and Innovation
  • Feasibility and Impact
  • Technical Execution
  • Scalability

Rewards and Recognition

  • Extended EON Reality Platform licenses for winners
  • Top 3 Teams of each category will receive awards
  • Recognition and visibility at national level
