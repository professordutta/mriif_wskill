Our Mission

At World Skills Academy, our mission is to serve as a launchpad for youth, equipping them with industry-relevant skills through state-of-the-art training facilities. We are committed to preparing the next generation of leaders and innovators who can compete and win at prestigious global platforms.

Our Vision

Our vision is to establish World Skills Academy as a premier center of excellence where youth are not only trained in world-class skills but are also prepared to bring home medals from prestigious WorldSkills competitions. We aim to create a launchpad for aspiring professionals who can showcase their talent on the global stage.

About WorldSkills Competition

The WorldSkills Competition is a prestigious global event showcasing excellence in vocational skills, first held in 1950 in Madrid, Spain. It brings together young professionals from around the world to compete in various skill categories, including technology, manufacturing, arts, and services.
